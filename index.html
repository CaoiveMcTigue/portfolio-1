Take care of yourself!

It is so important to care for yourself both mentally and physically. Doing so will lead toward a overall improved health and wellness. Looking after yourself is not selfish and should be part of everybody's daily routine, beginning to do so will not only help your wellbeing but partenial your loved ones also.

Mental Health

Self-care increases self awareness which is so important with a mental health issue of any scale. Incresing your self awareness can help you to reconise pattern that may trigger or increase symtoms relating to your illness.

So where do I start?

The simplest thing to do is, take time to do something you love or something that relaxes you and makes you happy. It can be anything from exercising, painting, watching your favourite show, having a bath and lighting some candles. It really doesn’t matter what it is once, you are happy and feel good about yourself afterwards.
